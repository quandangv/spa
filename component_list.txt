component list:
- core: give AI features
- generator: generates plasma
- accelerator: gives plasma speed
- energizer: gives plasma power
- turret: fires limited amount of plasma, requires free space ahead
- guiding turret: fires guided plasma (less capacity than turret)
- thruster: accelerates ship
- shield: protects surrounding components, regenerates if adjacent to generator
- armor: resists collision damage while having higher hp
